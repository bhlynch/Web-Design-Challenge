Summary: Latitude vs. Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, howerver, is the fact that the southern hemispher tends to be warmer this time of the year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at that time of the year this data was gathered.

Summary: Latitude vs. Humidity

There does not appear to be any significant correlation between latitude and humidity. It does apper that the lowest humidities are further away from the equator while cities close to the equator tend to be higher in humidity though, none of this appears in any conclusivive fashion.

Summary: Latitude vs. Cloudiness
